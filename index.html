Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

What areas do you service?

We primarily serve businesses in Warsaw and surrounding areas. For enterprise clients, we can arrange service throughout Poland. Remote support services are available nationwide.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

Response times vary by service level agreement. For our premium clients with critical issues, we guarantee a 2-hour onsite response time during business hours. Standard requests are typically addressed within 4 business hours.

Do you offer emergency after-hours support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency support for critical business systems. After-hours support is available for all managed service clients and can be arranged for project-based work as well.

What certifications do your technicians hold?

Our team holds current certifications including Microsoft Certified Professionals (MCP), Cisco Certified Network Associates (CCNA), CompTIA A+, Network+, and Security+, among others. We maintain ongoing training to stay current with technology advancements.

How does your 24-month warranty work?

Our warranty covers all labor and parts provided by us for 24 months from service completion. If any issue arises related to our work during this period, we'll repair or replace it at no cost. This excludes damage from accidents, misuse, or unrelated component failures.
